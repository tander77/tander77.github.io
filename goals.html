My Goals

Ever since I was a child, my dream has been to help in creating technologies that have been confined to the realm of science fiction.

To this end, I have studied Engineering at the University of Toronto. Soon after starting my program, I realized that my dream would take a combination of engineering and entrepreneurship.

As such, I enrolled in the Engineering Business Minor to gain the knowledge in this field that I would need.

It is my goal to one day become an entrepreneur in the fields of robotics and artificial intelligence and create new devices that both help people and make life more fun.
